(一四) 味 20 (14). Enjoyment (2)

如是我聞： Thus I have heard: t99.2.2c11 一時，佛住舍衞國、祇樹、給孤獨園。 One time, the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in Jeta’s Grove of Śrāvastī.

爾時，世尊告諸比丘： It was then that the Bhagavān addressed the monks, t99.2.2c12 「我昔於色味有求，有行。 “In the past, I had the pursuits and practices of enjoying form. 若於色味隨順覺，則於色味以智慧如實見。 When I realized the enjoyment of form as a result, I truly saw it with wisdom. 則 → () (宋, 元, 明) 如是，於受⋯想⋯行⋯識味有求，有行。 Thus, I had the pursuits and practices of enjoying feeling … conception … volition … consciousness. 若於受⋯想⋯行⋯識味隨順覺，則於識味以智慧如實見。 When I realized the enjoyment of feeling … conception … volition … consciousness as a result, I truly saw it with wisdom. 味(1st) → () (宋)

「諸比丘，我於色患有求，有行。 “Monks, I had the pursuits and practices of form’s trouble. t99.2.2c16 若於色患隨順覺，則於色患以智慧如實見。 When I realized form’s trouble as a result, I truly saw it with wisdom. 如是，受⋯想⋯行⋯識患有求，有行。 Thus, I had the pursuits and practices of feeling’s … conception’s … volition’s … consciousness’s trouble. 患 → () (宋) 若於識患隨順覺，則於識患以智慧如實見。 When I realized consciousness’s trouble as a result, I truly saw it with wisdom.

「諸比丘，我於色離有求，有行。 “Monks, I had the pursuits and practices of escaping form. t99.2.2c19 若於色隨順覺，則於色離以智慧如實見。 When I realized the escape from form as a result, I truly saw it with wisdom. 隨 → 離隨 (CBETA) 如是，受⋯想⋯行⋯識離有求，有行。 Thus, I had the pursuits and practices of escaping feeling … conception … volition … consciousness. 若於受⋯想⋯行⋯識離隨順覺，則於受⋯想⋯行⋯識離以智慧如實見。 When I realized the escape from consciousness as a result, I truly saw it with wisdom.

「諸比丘，我於五受陰不如實知味是味⋯患是患⋯離是離者，我於諸天若魔、若梵、沙門、婆羅門、天人衆中，不脱，不離，不出，永住顛倒。 “Monks, had I not truly known the enjoyment of the five acquired aggregates to be enjoyment … trouble to be trouble … escape to be escape, I would have forever stood in delusion among the gods, Māra, Brahmā, ascetics, and priests in the assemblies of gods and humans, and I wouldn’t have been freed from, parted with, and escaped from [these aggregates]. t99.2.2c23 不能自證得阿耨多羅三藐三菩提。 I couldn’t have realized my attainment of the unsurpassed and complete awakening.

「諸比丘，我以如實知五受陰味是味⋯患是患⋯離是離，我於諸天人若魔、若梵、沙門、婆羅門、天人衆中以脱，以離，以出，永不住顛倒。 “Monks, I did truly know the enjoyment of the five acquired aggregates to be enjoyment … trouble to be trouble … escape to be escape, and I never again stood in delusion among the gods, Māra, Brahmā, ascetics, and priests in the assemblies of gods and humans, and I was freed from, parted with, and escaped from [these aggregates]. t99.2.2c27 人(1st) → () (宋, 元, 明) 以脱 → 已脱 (元, 明) 以離 → 已離 (元, 明) 以出 → 已出 (元, 明) 能自證得阿耨多羅三藐三菩提。 I could realize my attainment of the unsurpassed and complete awakening.”

時，諸比丘聞佛所説歡喜，奉行。 When the monks heard what the Buddha taught, they rejoiced and approved. t99.2.3a2
