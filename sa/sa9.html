• 雜阿含經 9 The Related Discourses

（九） 厭離 15 (9). Teaching Disillusionment

如是我聞： Thus I have heard: t99.2.2a2 一時，佛住舍衛國，祇樹，給孤獨園。 One time, the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in Jeta’s Grove of Śrāvastī.

爾時，世尊告諸比丘： It was then that the Bhagavān addressed the monks, t99.2.2a3 「色無常。 “Form is impermanent. 無常即苦，苦即非我，非我者亦非我所。 What’s impermanent is painful, what’s painful is not self, and what’s not self doesn’t belong to self, either. 如是觀者名真實正觀。 Such observation is called true and correct observation. 如是，受⋯想⋯行⋯識無常。 Thus, feeling … conception … volition … consciousness is impermanent. 無常即苦，苦即非我，非我者亦非我所。 What’s impermanent is painful, what’s painful is not self, and what’s not self doesn’t belong to self, either. 者 → () (宋, 元, 明) 如是觀者名真實觀。 Such an observation is called a true and correct observation. 實 → 實正 (宋, 元, 明, yinshun)

「聖弟子如是觀者厭於色。 “Noble disciples who thus observe form become disillusioned with it. t99.2.2a7 厭受⋯想⋯行⋯識。 They become disillusioned with sensation … conception … volition … consciousness. 厭故不樂。 Because they’re disillusioned, they don’t enjoy them. 不樂故得解脫。 Because they don’t enjoy them, they attain liberation. 解脫者真實智生： For someone who’s liberated, this true knowledge arises: 『我生已盡，梵行已立，所作已作。 ‘My births have been ended, the religious practice has been established, and the task has been accomplished. 自知不受後有。』」 I myself know that I won’t be subject to a later existence.’”

時，諸比丘聞佛所說，歡喜奉行。 When the monks heard what the Buddha taught, they rejoiced and approved. t99.2.2a10
