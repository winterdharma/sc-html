（七） 於色喜樂 10 (7). Delighting in Form

如是我聞： Thus I have heard: t99.2.1c11 一時，佛住舍衛國，祇樹，給孤獨園。 One time, the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in Jeta’s Grove of Śrāvastī.

爾時，世尊告諸比丘： It was then that the Bhagavān addressed the monks, t99.2.1c12 「於色愛喜者則於苦愛喜。 “When one delights in form, they delight in suffering. 於苦愛喜者則於苦不得解脫。 When one delights in suffering, they can’t be liberated from suffering. 如是，受⋯想⋯行⋯識愛喜者則愛喜苦。 Thus, when one delights in feeling … conception … volition … consciousness, they delight in suffering. 愛喜苦者則於苦不得解脫。 When one delights in suffering, they can’t be liberated from suffering.

「諸比丘，於色不愛喜者則不喜於苦。 “Monks, when someone doesn’t delight in form, they don’t delight in suffering. t99.2.1c15 不喜於苦者則於苦得解脫。 When someone doesn’t delight in suffering, they can be liberated from suffering. 於苦(2nd) → 苦 (宋, 元, 明) 如是，受⋯想⋯行⋯識不愛喜者則不喜於苦。 Thus, when someone doesn’t delight in feeling … conception … volition … consciousness, they don’t delight in suffering. 不喜於苦者則於苦得解脫。」 When someone doesn’t delight in suffering, they can be liberated from suffering.”

時，諸比丘聞佛所說，歡喜奉行。 When the monks heard what the Buddha taught, they rejoiced and approved. t99.2.1c19
