• 雜阿含經 15 The Related Discourses

(一五) 使 21 (15). Tendencies

如是我聞： Thus I have heard: t99.2.3a6 一時，佛住舍衞國、祇樹、給孤獨園。 One time, the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in Jeta’s Grove of Śrāvastī.

爾時，有異比丘來詣佛所，稽首佛足，却住一面。 At the time, a certain monk came to the Buddha, bowed his head at the Buddha’s feet, and withdrew to stand to one side. t99.2.3a7 爾 → () (宋, 元, 明) 白佛言： He said, 「善哉，世尊！今當爲我略説法要。 “It’d be excellent, Bhagavān, if you would briefly explain the teaching for me now. 我聞法已，當獨一靜處，修不放逸。 After hearing the teaching, I’ll go alone to a quiet place and cultivate it earnestly. 修不放逸已，當復思惟： After cultivating it earnestly, I’ll again contemplate: 『所以善男子出家，剃除鬚髮，身著法服，信家，非家，出家爲究竟無上梵行，現法作證： ‘It’s because a good man leaves home, shaves his hair, puts on the Dharma robes, becomes faithful, goes homeless, and leaves home to train in the ultimate and unsurpassed religious life that he realizes in the present life: 「我生已盡，梵行已立，所作已作。 “My births have been ended, the religious practice has been established, and the task has been accomplished. 自知不受後有。」』」 I myself know that I won’t be subject to a later existence.”’”

爾時，世尊告彼比丘： The Bhagavān told that monk, t99.2.3a13 「善哉，善哉！比丘，快説此言： “Good, monk! Good! You’ve expressed yourself gladly, saying, 『云當爲我略説法要？ ‘Would you briefly explain the teaching for me now? 我聞法已，獨一靜處修不放逸⋯乃至⋯ After I’ve heard the teaching, I’ll go alone to a quiet place and cultivate it earnestly … 「自知不受後有。」』 “I myself know that I won’t be subject to a later existence.”’ 如是説耶？」 Is this what you’ve asked?”

比丘白佛： The monk said to the Buddha, t99.2.3a16 「如是，世尊。」 “So it is, Bhagavān.”

佛告比丘： The Buddha told the monk, t99.2.3a17 「諦聽，諦聽！善思念之。 “Listen closely! Listen closely, and consider it well. 當爲汝説。 I will explain this for you. 比丘，若隨使使者，即隨使死。 Monk, if someone follows the tendencies, then they’re following death. 若隨死者，爲取所縛。 If someone follows death, then they’re bound by grasping. 比丘，若不隨使使，則不隨使死。 Monk, if someone doesn’t follow the tendencies, then they aren’t following death. 不隨使死者，則於取解脱。」 Not following death, they are freed from grasping.”

比丘白佛： The monk said to the Buddha, t99.2.3a21 「知已，世尊！知已，善逝！」 “I’ve understood, Bhagavān! I’ve understood, Sugata!”

佛告比丘： The Buddha asked the monk, t99.2.3a21 「汝云何於我略説法中廣解其義？」 “How exactly do you understand the meaning of my brief explanation of the teaching?”

比丘白佛言： The monk said to the Buddha, t99.2.3a22 「世尊，色隨使使，色隨使死。 “Bhagavān, form follows the tendencies, and form follows death. 隨使使隨使死者，則爲取所縛。 Someone who follows the tendencies and follows death is bound by grasping. 如是，受⋯想⋯行⋯識隨使使隨使死。 Thus, feeling … conception … volition … consciousness follows the tendencies, and consciousness follows death. 隨使使隨使死者，爲取所縛。 Someone who follows the tendencies and follows death is bound by grasping.

「世尊，若色不隨使使，不隨使死。 “Bhagavān, when form doesn’t follow the tendencies, it doesn’t follow death. t99.2.3a26 不隨使使不隨使死者，則於取解脱。 Someone who doesn’t follow the tendencies and doesn’t follow death is freed from grasping. 如是，受⋯想⋯行⋯識不隨使使不隨使死。 Thus, if feeling … conception … volition … consciousness doesn’t follow the tendencies, it doesn’t follow death. 不隨使使不隨使死者，則於取解脱。 Someone who doesn’t follow the tendencies and doesn’t follow death is freed from grasping. 如是，世尊，略説法中廣解其義。」 Thus do I understand the Bhagavān’s brief explanation of the teaching.”

佛告比丘： The Buddha told the monk, t99.2.3b1 「善哉，善哉！比丘，於我略説法中廣解其義。 “Good, good! That’s a detailed understanding of the meaning of my brief explanation. 所以者何？ Why is that? 色隨使使，隨使死。 When form follows the tendencies, it follows death. 隨使使隨使死者，則爲取所縛。 Someone who follows the tendencies and follows death is bound by grasping. 如是，受⋯想⋯行⋯識隨使使，隨使死。 Thus, when feeling … conception … volition … consciousness follows the tendencies, it follows death. 隨使使隨使死者，則爲取所縛。 Someone who follows the tendencies and follows death is bound by grasping.

「比丘，色不隨使使，不隨使死。 “Monk, when form doesn’t follow the tendencies, it doesn’t follow death. t99.2.3b5 不隨使使不隨使死者，則於取解脱。 Someone who doesn’t follow the tendencies and doesn’t follow death is freed from grasping. 如是，受⋯想⋯行⋯識不隨使使，不隨使死。 Thus, when feeling … conception … volition … consciousness doesn’t follow the tendencies, it doesn’t follow death. 不隨使使不隨使死者，則於取解脱。」 Someone who doesn’t follow the tendencies and doesn’t follow death is freed from grasping.”

時，彼比丘聞佛所説，心大歡喜。 When that monk heard what the Buddha taught, he felt great joy. t99.2.3b9 禮佛而退。 He bowed to the Buddha and withdrew. 獨在靜處，精勤修習，住不放逸。 He dwelled alone in a quiet place, diligently trained, and lived earnestly. 精勤修習，住不放逸已，思惟： After diligently training and living earnestly, he contemplated this: 「所以善男子出家，剃除鬚髮，身著法服，信家，非家，出家⋯乃至⋯ “It’s because a good man leaves home, shaves his hair, puts on the Dharma robes, becomes faithful, goes homeless, and leaves home … 『自知不受後有。』」 I myself know that I won’t be subject to a later existence.’”

時，彼比丘即成羅漢，心得解脱。 Then that monk became an arhat, and his mind was liberated. t99.2.3b13
