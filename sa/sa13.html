• 雜阿含經 13 The Related Discourses

（一三） 味 19 (13). Enjoyment

如是我聞： Thus I have heard: t99.2.2b15 一時，佛住舍衛國，祇樹，給孤獨園。 One time, the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in Jeta’s Grove of Śrāvastī.

爾時，世尊告諸比丘： It was then that the Bhagavān addressed the monks, t99.2.2b16 「若眾生於色不味者，則不染於色。 “If sentient beings didn’t enjoy form, then they wouldn’t be stained by it. 以眾生於色味故，則有染著。 It’s because they do enjoy form that sentient beings are stained and attached to it. 如是眾生於受⋯想⋯行⋯識不味者，彼眾生則不染於識。 Thus, when sentient beings don’t enjoy feeling … conception … volition … consciousness, they aren’t stained by them. 識(2nd) → 受⋯想⋯行⋯識 (yinshun) 以眾生味受⋯想⋯行⋯識故，彼眾生染著於識。 It’s because sentient beings do enjoy feeling … conception … volition … consciousness that they are stained and attached to them. 識(2nd) → 受⋯想⋯行⋯識 (yinshun)

「諸比丘，若色於眾生不為患者，彼諸眾生不應厭色。 “Monks, if form weren’t trouble for sentient beings, then sentient beings wouldn’t become disillusioned with it. t99.2.2b20 以色為眾生患故，彼諸眾生則厭於色。 It’s because form is trouble for sentient beings that they do become disillusioned with it. 如是受⋯想⋯行⋯識不為患者，彼諸眾生不應厭識。 Thus, if feeling … conception … volition … consciousness wasn’t trouble for sentient beings, they wouldn’t become disillusioned with it. 識(2nd) → 受⋯想⋯行⋯識 (yinshun) 以受⋯想⋯行⋯識為眾生患故，彼諸眾生則厭於識。 It’s because feeling … perception … volition … consciousness is trouble for sentient beings that they do become disillusioned with it. 識(2nd) → 受⋯想⋯行⋯識 (yinshun)

「諸比丘，若色於眾生無出離者，彼諸眾生不應出離於色。 “Monks, if sentient beings had no escape from form, then sentient beings wouldn’t escape from it. t99.2.2b25 以色於眾生有出離故，彼諸眾生出離於色。 It’s because sentient beings have an escape from form that they do escape from it. 如是受⋯想⋯行⋯識於眾生無出離者，彼諸眾生不應出離於識。 Thus, if sentient beings had no escape from feeling … conception … volition … consciousness, they wouldn’t escape from it. 識(2nd) → 受⋯想⋯行⋯識 (yinshun) 以受⋯想⋯行⋯識於眾生有出離故，彼諸眾生出離於識。 It’s because sentient beings have an escape from feeling … conception … volition … consciousness that they do escape from it. 識(2nd) → 受⋯想⋯行⋯識 (yinshun)

「諸比丘，若我於此五受陰不如實知味是味⋯⋯患是患⋯⋯離是離者， “Monks, if I didn’t truly know the enjoyment of the five acquired aggregates to be enjoyment … trouble to be trouble … escape to be escape, I wouldn’t be freed, escaped, and parted with [bondage]. t99.2.2c1 我於諸天，若魔若梵，沙門，婆羅門，天，人眾中不脫，不出，不離，永住顛倒，亦不能自證得阿耨多羅三藐三菩提。 I would have forever stood in delusion among gods, Māra, Brahmā, ascetics, and priests in the assemblies of gods and humans, and I couldn’t have realized my attainment of the unsurpassed and complete awakening.

「諸比丘，我以如實知此五受陰味是味⋯患是患⋯離是離故， “Monks, it’s because I truly knew the enjoyment of the five acquired aggregates to be enjoyment … trouble to be trouble … escape to be escape that I was freed, escaped, parted, and liberated from bondage. t99.2.2c5 我於諸天，若魔若梵，沙門，婆羅門，天，人眾中，自證得脫，得出，得離，得解脫結縛，永不住顛倒，亦能自證得阿耨多羅三藐三菩提。」 I never again stood in delusion among gods, Māra, Brahmā, ascetics, and priests in the assemblies of gods and humans, and I could realize my attainment of the unsurpassed and complete awakening.” 自證(1st) → () (yinshun)

時，諸比丘聞佛所說，歡喜奉行。 When the monks heard what the Buddha taught, they rejoiced and approved. t99.2.2c9
