• 雜阿含經 8 The Related Discourses

（八） 過去四種説 11-14 (8). The Present Is Impermanent, Painful, Empty, and Not Self

如是我聞： Thus I have heard: t99.2.1c22 一時，佛住舍衛國，祇樹，給孤獨園。 One time, the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in Jeta’s Grove of Śrāvastī.

爾時，世尊告諸比丘： It was then that the Bhagavān addressed the monks, t99.2.1c23 「過去、未來色無常，況現在色？ “Past and future form are [11] impermanent … [12] painful … [13] empty … [14] not self, so why wouldn’t that be for present form? 聖弟子如是觀者不顧過去色，不欲未來色。 Noble disciples who thus observe them don’t look back to past form or wish for future form. 不欲 → 不欣 (元, 明) 於現在色厭、離欲、正向滅盡。 Regarding present form, they become disillusioned, free of desire, and properly headed for its cessation.

「如是，過去、未來受⋯想⋯行⋯識無常，況現在識？ “Thus, past and future feeling … conception … volition … consciousness are [11] impermanent … [12] painful … [13] empty … [14] not self, so why wouldn’t that be for present consciousness? t99.2.1c26 識(2nd) → 受⋯想⋯行⋯識 (yinshun) 聖弟子如是觀者不顧過去識，不欣未來識。 Noble disciples who thus observe them don’t look back to past consciousness or wish for future consciousness. 於現在識厭、離欲、正向滅盡。」 Regarding present consciousness, they become disillusioned, free of desire, and properly headed for its cessation.”

時，諸比丘聞佛所說，歡喜奉行。 When the monks heard what the Buddha taught, they rejoiced and approved. t99.2.1c29
