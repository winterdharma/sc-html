• 中阿含經 2 The Medium Discourses

（二） 晝度樹經 2. The Pārijāta Tree

我聞如是： Thus I have heard: t26.1.422a19 一時，佛遊舍衛國，在勝林、給孤獨園。 One time, the Buddha traveled to the country of Śrāvastī and stayed at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in Jeta’s Grove.

The Seasons of the Pārijāta Tree t26.1.422a20

爾時，世尊告諸比丘： It was then that the Bhagavān addressed the monks, t26.1.422a20 「若三十三天晝度樹葉萎黄，是時三十三天悦樂、歡喜： “When the leaves of the Trāyastriṃśa Heaven’s pārijāta tree turn yellow, the Trāyastriṃśa gods are delighted and rejoice: ‘It won’t be long before the pārijāta tree’s leaves begin to fall!’ 『晝度樹葉不久當落！』

「復次，三十三天晝度樹葉已落，是時三十三天悦樂、歡喜： “Furthermore, once the leaves of the Trāyastriṃśa Heaven’s pārijāta tree have fallen, the Trāyastriṃśa gods are delighted and rejoice: t26.1.422a22 『晝度樹葉不久當還生！』 ‘It won’t be long before the pārijāta tree’s leaves grow back!’

「復次，三十三天晝度樹葉已還生，是時三十三天悦樂、歡喜： “Furthermore, once the leaves of the Trāyastriṃśa Heaven’s pārijāta tree have grown back, the Trāyastriṃśa gods are delighted and rejoice: t26.1.422a24 『晝度樹不久當生網！』 ‘It won’t be long before the pārijāta tree grows a web of buds!’

「復次，三十三天晝度樹已生網，是時三十三天悦樂、歡喜： “Furthermore, once the Trāyastriṃśa Heaven’s pārijāta tree has grown a web of buds, the Trāyastriṃśa gods are delighted and rejoice: t26.1.422a26 『晝度樹不久當生如鳥喙！』 ‘It won’t be long before the pārijāta tree’s buds will grow like bird beaks!’

「復次，三十三天晝度樹已生如鳥喙，是時三十三天悦樂、歡喜： “Furthermore, once the Trāyastriṃśa Heaven’s pārijāta tree has grown buds like bird beaks, the Trāyastriṃśa gods are delighted and rejoice, t26.1.422a28 『晝度樹不久當開如鉢！』 ‘It won’t be long before the pārijāta tree’s buds open like bowls!’

「復次，三十三天晝度樹已開如鉢，是時三十三天悦樂、歡喜： “Furthermore, once the buds of the Trāyastriṃśa Heaven’s pārijāta tree have opened like bowls, the Trāyastriṃśa gods are delighted and rejoice: t26.1.422b1 『晝度樹不久當盡敷開！』 ‘It won’t be long before the pārijāta tree will be in full bloom!’

「若晝度樹已盡敷開，光所照、色所映、香所熏周百由延。 “Once the pārijāta tree is in full bloom, the light that it shines, the colors that it reflects, and the fragrance that it produces have a range of a hundred yojanas. t26.1.422b2 是時三十三天於中夏四月，以天五欲、功徳具足而自娯樂。 During the four months of summer, the Trāyastriṃśa gods entertain themselves with the five heavenly pleasures and the virtues with which they’re endowed. 是謂三十三天於晝度樹下集會，娯樂也。 This is called the gathering under the Trāyastriṃśa Heaven’s pārijāta tree.

The Seasons of the Noble Disciple t26.1.422b6

「如是義，聖弟子亦復爾。 “The meaning of the noble disciple is like this, too. t26.1.422b6 思念出家，是時聖弟子名爲『葉黄』。 When they consider leaving home, the disciple is called ‘leaves turning yellow.’ 猶三十三天晝度樹葉萎黄也。 They’re like the Trāyastriṃśa Heaven’s pārijāta tree when its leaves turn yellow.

「復次，聖弟子剃除鬚髮，著袈裟衣，至信，捨家，無家，學道。是時，聖弟子名爲『葉落』。 “Furthermore, when they cut off their hair, put on the reddish-brown robes, attain faith, leave home, go into homelessness, and train on the path, then the noble disciple is called ‘falling leaves.’ t26.1.422b8 猶三十三天晝度樹葉落也。 They’re like the Trāyastriṃśa Heaven’s pārijāta tree dropping its leaves.

「復次，聖弟子離欲，離惡、不善之法。 “Furthermore, the noble disciple parts with desire and bad and unskillful things. t26.1.422b11 有覺、有觀，離生喜樂，得初禪成就遊。 With perception and examination, this seclusion produces joy and happiness, and they attain the accomplishment of the first dhyāna. 是時聖弟子名『葉還生』。 The noble disciple then is called ‘growing back leaves.’ 猶三十三天晝度樹葉還生也。 They’re like the Trāyastriṃśa Heaven’s pārijāta tree growing back its leaves.

「復次，聖弟子覺、觀已息，内靜、一心。 “Furthermore, once perception and examination have stopped, the noble disciple has an inner stillness and unified mind. t26.1.422b14 靜 → 靖 (宋, 元, 明) 無覺、無觀，定生喜樂，得第二禪成就遊。 Without perception or examination, this samādhi produces joy and happiness, and they attain the accomplishment of the second dhyāna. 是時聖弟子名爲『生網』。 The noble disciple then is called ‘growing a web of buds.’ 猶三十三天晝度樹生網也。 They’re like the Trāyastriṃśa Heaven’s pārijāta tree growing a web of buds.

「復次，聖弟子離於喜欲，捨、無求遊。 “Furthermore, the noble disciple parts with desire for joy, and they abide with equanimity and pursue nothing. t26.1.422b17 正念、正智而身覺樂，謂聖所説，聖所捨、念、樂住、空。 With right mindfulness and right knowledge, they personally experience the happiness that’s described by noble people as equanimity, mindfulness, happy abiding, and emptiness. 得第三禪成就遊。 They attain the accomplishment of the third dhyāna. 是時聖弟子名『生如鳥喙』。 The noble disciple then is called ‘growing buds like bird beaks.’ 猶三十三天晝度樹如鳥喙也。 They’re like the Trāyastriṃśa Heaven’s pārijāta tree growing buds like bird beaks.

「復次，聖弟子樂滅，苦滅。 “Furthermore, the noble disciple ceases to feel pleasure ceases and ceases to feel pain. t26.1.422b20 喜、憂本已滅，不苦、不樂。 The basis of joy and sorrow having ceased, they’re neither discomforted nor delighted. 捨、念、清淨，得第四禪成就遊。 Equanimous, mindful, and pure, they attain the accomplishment of the fourth dhyāna. 是時聖弟子名『生如鉢』。 The noble disciple then is called ‘opening like a bowl.’ 猶三十三天晝度樹如鉢也。 They’re like the Trāyastriṃśa Heaven’s pārijāta tree when its buds open like bowls.

「復次，聖弟子諸漏已盡，心解脱、慧解脱。 “Furthermore, once the noble disciple’s contaminants have ended, their mind is liberated, and their wisdom is liberated. t26.1.422b23 於現法中，自知、自覺自作證成就遊： In the present life, they know and recognize for themselves the accomplishment of this realization: 『生已盡，梵行已立，所作已辦。 ‘Birth has been ended, the religious practice has been established, the task has been accomplished. 不更受有，知如眞。』 I truly know that I’m no longer subject to existence.’ 是時聖弟子名『盡敷開』。 The noble disciple then is called ‘in full bloom.’ 猶三十三天晝度樹盡敷開也。 They’re like the Trāyastriṃśa Heaven’s pārijāta tree when it’s in full bloom.

Conclusion t26.1.422b28

「彼爲漏盡阿羅訶比丘。 “He becomes an arhat monk whose contaminants have ended. t26.1.422b28 三十三天集在善法正殿，咨嗟、稱歎： The Trāyastriṃśa gods assemble in the Sudharmā Meeting Hall, sighing admiringly and praising them, 『某尊弟子於某村邑剃除鬚髮，著袈裟衣，至信，捨家，無家學道。 ‘Some worthy disciple at some town cut off their hair, put on the reddish-brown robes, became faithful, left home, went into homelessness, and trained on the path. 諸漏已盡，心解脱、慧解脱。 Once their contaminants had ended, their mind was liberated, and their wisdom was liberated. 於現法中，自知、自覺自作證成就遊。 In the present life, they know and recognize for themselves the accomplishment of this realization: 「生已盡，梵行已立，所作已辦。 “Birth has been ended, the religious practice has been established, and the task has been accomplished. 不更受有，知如眞。」』 I truly know that I won’t be subject to another existence.”’

「是謂漏盡阿羅訶共集會也，如三十三天晝度樹下共集會也。」 “This is called the assembly that coincides with the end of an arhat’s contaminants, which is like the gathering held under the Trāyastriṃśa Heaven’s pārijāta tree.” t26.1.422c4

佛説如是。 This is what the Buddha said. t26.1.422c6 彼諸比丘聞佛所説歡喜，奉行。 Those monks who heard what the Buddha taught rejoiced and approved.
